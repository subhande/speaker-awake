
# Speaker Awake

Keep your speakers awake with this simple utility. It prevents your speakers from going into sleep mode, ensuring uninterrupted audio playback.

Functionality:
- Periodically sends a signal to the speakers to keep them awake.
- Configurable interval for sending the signal.
- Lightweight and easy to use.
- OS: MacOS - Should be Optimized and Super Lightweight
- Available on MacOS Top Bar for easy access and control.
- Good Looking and Minimalistic UI.
- Tech Stack: Most Optimized and Lightweight Tech Stack for MacOS Applications.
